Population Variance Formula
Var(X) = omega ^ 2 = 1/N * sum of (x - mean) ^ 2
mean = mean of population
N = size of population

Sample Variance Formula
s^2 = sum of (x - mean (symbol = x have - over head)) ^ 2 / n - 1

Central Limit Theorem (Discrete Random Variable)
u = n*p

omega ^ 2 = n*p * (1 - p)

Central Limit Theorem (Continuous Random Variable)
mean = 1/n * sum of (E[x]) = 1/n * n * E[x]

Variance
var = Var(1/n * sum of (Xi)) 
        = 1/(n ^ 2) * sum of (Var(Xi))
            = 1/(n ^ 2) * n * Var(X)
                = Var(X)/n
